# Judo Command Center - User Guide

## Introduction
Use this application to manage your Judo association's athletes, tournaments, and administrative details. The **Judo Command Center** provides a centralized dashboard for tracking athlete progress, managing tournament rosters, and maintaining club affiliations.

---

## 1. Getting Started

### Installation
Ensure the application is installed on your Windows machine. Launch "Judo Command Center" from your desktop or start menu.

### Initial Setup (Regency Branding)
Upon first login, customize the application for your specific region:
1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Branding**.
2. Enter your **Kabupatan / City Name**.
3. Upload your **Regency Logo**.
   * This logo and name will appear in the sidebar and on official reports.

---

## 2. Dashboard
The Dashboard is your command center, providing a high-level overview of your organization.

* **KPI Cards**: Monitor key metrics like *Total Pool*, *Competitive Pool*, and *Gender Ratio* at a glance.
* **Pool Health**: A visual indicator (Online/Offline) showing the system status.
* **Medallion Summary**: An interactive chart tracking medal achievements over user-selected years.
* **Quick Actions**: Use shortcuts to immediately **Add New Athlete** or **Manage Tournaments**.

---

## 3. Athlete Management
Manage your athlete database effectively.

### List View
* Navigate to the **Athletes** tab.
* Use the search bar to find athletes by name or club.
* Filter the list by connection status or other criteria.

### Athlete Profile
Click on an athlete to view or edit their details:
* **Personal Info**: Update Name, Date of Birth (Age Category is auto-calculated), Weight Class, and Rank.
* **Activity Status**: Set status to *Active*, *Intermittent*, or *Inactive* to track participation.
* **Parental Information**: Record guardian contact details for minor athletes.
* **Images**: Upload a profile picture for easy identification.

### Tournament History
* **Automated Tracking**: When you add an athlete to a tournament roster, a history entry is *automatically created*.
* **Timeline View**: View a chronological timeline of all tournaments attended.
* **Manual Entry**: You can manually add past tournaments if they were not managed within this system.

---

## 4. Tournament Management
Organize and run Judo tournaments.

### Creating a Tournament
1. Go to **Tournaments** and click **New Tournament**.
2. Enter **Name**, **Date**, and **Location**.
3. Select a **Ruleset**. 
   * *Note:* Once a tournament is created with a ruleset, the ruleset is "frozen" for that tournament to preserve historical integrity.

### Managing Rosters
Inside a tournament:
1. **Define Weight Classes**: If your ruleset allows, define specific weight classes for the age categories in this tournament.
2. **Add Athletes**:
   * Select an **Age Category** and **Weight Class**.
   * The list filters to show eligible athletes.
   * Click **Add** to place them on the roster.
   * **Validation**: The system warns you if an athlete's age or weight does not match the selected category.

### PDF Export
* Click **Export PDF** to generate a printable summary of the tournament roster for offline use or official records.

---

## 5. Settings
Configure the system to your rules.

* **Clubs**: Add and manage the list of affiliated Dojo/Clubs in your regency.
* **Rulesets**: Define competition rules, including Age Categories (e.g., U15, Senior) and their specific parameters.
* **Branding**: Update your storage of official logos and naming conventions.
